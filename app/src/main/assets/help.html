About App

This is a minimal implement of Google Classroom, the main idea behind this is to make Classroom Announcement available to Students when Offline.
Since the idea for the is Clear enough therefore there's no Support for Course Administrators
Certain features such as Creating Announcement in Classrooms have been disabled

Limitations

Even though the main purpose of the App is to sync Course state for Classroom students, Users should be aware of the following liumitations:

  • Comments on Announcement ain't available
  • Only Course Administrators can View all Students in a given Classroom
  • CourseWorks can't be submitted directly from the App

Any other feature apart from these was only as a result of mere design Choice

Google Drive Document Requesting Permission
When the Google Drive Account chooser dialog appears, open Attachments only with E-mail accounts used for the given Classroom class.
In case you ain't signed into the device with the Google Account used for the Classroom Course, consider signing into Google Drive with the Account.

App moving to top when loading Announcement

Once again this is only a mere design choice. The App automatically attempt to sync updates, and when updates are available it moves to the top and updates the Content of the Current View.

Sign Out or Revoke Access
Consider signing out of the app only for simple cases such as switching between Google Accounts.
Revoking Classroom Client access disconnects this Application with your Google Account, Signing back into the app would require you to grant the Scopes required by the App again.
